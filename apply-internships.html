Winter / Summer Internships

UAII internships are designed for undergraduate (and early graduate) students who want hands-on experience in Urban AI, simulation, data-driven urban systems, and research software engineering.

Who should apply

  • Undergraduate students (or early graduate students) interested in AI, simulation, and urban systems
  • Comfortable with at least one programming language (Python recommended)
  • Interest in building prototypes, running experiments, and writing research code

Period & format

  • Period: Every Winter & Summer vacation (exact dates vary)
  • Format: Full-time (recommended) / Part-time (limited cases)
  • Location: KAIST (Daejeon) / Remote (case-by-case)

Note: Internship schedules and availability may depend on project timelines and advisor capacity.

Typical projects

Data pipeline & visualization Urban simulation / digital twin ML / CV for urban sensing Research tooling & web demos

How to apply

  1. Prepare CV(Curriculum Vitae)
  2. Email your documents to urban@kaist.ac.kr with the subject: [Application] Internship - Your_Name
  3. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview and project matching.
