Research Professors / Postdoctoral Fellows

We welcome Ph.D. holders who are excited to lead and contribute to research in Urban AI, simulation, urban representation, and data-driven infrastructure systems.

What we look for

  • Ph.D. in a relevant field (Civil/Urban/Transportation/CS/AI, etc.)
  • Strong publication record or demonstrated project leadership
  • Ability to work collaboratively across disciplines (AI × urban systems)
  • Experience with research software (Python, ML stacks, simulation tooling) is a plus

Research focus

Urban AI Urban Representation Urban Simulation

Depending on ongoing projects, there may be opportunities to lead sub-projects, mentor students, and contribute to proposal writing.

Application materials

  • CV (including publications)

How to apply

  1. Prepare the CV(Curriculum Vitae)
  2. Email to urban@kaist.ac.kr with the subject: [Application] Postdoc - Your_Name
  3. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview.
