Responsibilities

  • Lead and participate in Urban AI Institute–led research projects.
  • Plan, win, and manage collaborative grants and industry partnerships (domestic & international).
  • Support institute operations and general administrative tasks as needed.
  • Develop core Urban AI technologies for sustainable, human-centered city systems.

Research Assistant Professor

Qualifications

  • Ph.D. in a relevant field (e.g., AI/ML, Computer Vision, Data Science, Transportation/Urban Systems, GIS).
  • Strong publication record and clear research vision aligned with Urban AI.
  • Ability to mentor students and collaborate across disciplines.
  • Excellent written and oral communication (English; Korean a plus).

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience as PI/Co-PI or key personnel on funded projects, proposal writing, and project management.
  • Top-tier journal/conference publications and invited talks.
  • Hands-on skills in Python and modern ML stacks (e.g., PyTorch/JAX), data engineering, and MLOps.
  • Active international collaboration network and leadership experience.

Postdoctoral Researcher

Qualifications

  • Ph.D. in AI/ML, CV, Data Science, Transportation/Urban Engineering, GIS, or related areas.
  • Demonstrated ability to publish in reputable venues and to drive independent research.
  • Proficiency in Python and end-to-end research workflows (experiments, reproducibility, documentation).
  • Strong collaboration and communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with geospatial intelligence, mobility systems, or foundation models for urban data.
  • Background in large-scale datasets, cloud computing, or high-performance training pipelines.
  • Prior involvement in multi-institution or industry-sponsored projects.

Research Associate

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree (or equivalent experience) in CS/AI, Data/Geo-Informatics, Transportation/Urban, or related fields.
  • Solid coding skills in Python; familiarity with data analysis and ML basics.
  • Clear written/oral communication; ability to organize experiments and datasets.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with GIS tools (e.g., QGIS, GeoPandas), visualization, and data pipelines.
  • Hands-on model prototyping (PyTorch) and experiment tracking (e.g., Weights & Biases).
  • Experience coordinating academic–industry collaborations.
